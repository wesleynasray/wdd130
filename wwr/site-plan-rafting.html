Overview

Purpose

Provide a quick glimpse of our rafting trips, motivating visitors to buy tickets.

Audience

Who are the target customers?
Groups of young adult men and women with stressful work routines who share workplace or friendship.

What do they want?
A thriling escape of stress and work routine, to exercise courage, or even to improve teamworking by overcoming safe, but exciting, natural obstacles.

What questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
Is the tax feasible for each member of the group? Are the trips safe, but exciting? How may I reserve a trip and which dates and time are available?
